How do I add an anime to my list?

You can add anime to your list from the Discover or Seasonal pages. Simply click the bookmark icon on any anime card to add it to your "Plan to Watch" list.

How do I move an anime to a different list?

On the "My List" page, click the "Move" button on any anime card. A dialog will appear allowing you to select a new list for the anime.
